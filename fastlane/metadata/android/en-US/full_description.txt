NetWatch is a privacy-first Android diagnostics app that records network transitions, outages, anomalies, and on-demand speed tests with timestamped context.

The app uses local Room databases to store comprehensive historical telemetry, giving you indisputable proof of cellular dead zones and regional networking stability.

Highlights:
- Lightweight passive background monitoring
- Fast on-demand heavy diagnostics (download/upload speed tests)
- Historical timeline log mapping exact signal drops with lat/lng geolocation coordinates
- Export daily/weekly stats locally or generate custom PDF reports
- Dual-SIM support (reads active data-routing SIM characteristics)
- OpenStreetMap-based coverage map with optional offline tile caching

NetWatch requires absolutely zero network permissions to report to arbitrary servers. All analytics are stored entirely on-device and can only be extracted via manual exports by the user.
